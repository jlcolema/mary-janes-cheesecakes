Hi,

I hope this email finds you well! I wanted to introduce myself, and my company, Mary Jane’s Cheesecakes. We are a local company specializing in a new, totally unique type of cheesecake... the “Country Cheesecake”. We are the only company in the county, probably the nation, and most certainly the world, that makes this type of cheesecake. Currently, we have 7 “Country” cheesecakes, and 9, more traditional, country inspired flavors, and they are unabashedly simple and clean! We don’t add any preservatives or fillers... no egg or flour in our fillings... or anything that doesn’t absolutely need to be there. They are pre-cut with 8 or 10 slices, can be served chilled, frozen, or deep fried, and garnished or finished by your staff, to reflect your style. A clean canvas for your culinary creativity!

Cheesecake is one of the most ordered restaurant desserts in America, at 29%... second only to pie, at 30%. Our focus is on the wholesale market, and whether it’s by the case, pallet, or truckload... we can do it all. We understand that cost is a concern... but it can’t be the only one. If you take pride in your establishment and in the food you serve, and I can only assume that you do, that pride should also extend to your desserts... even if you don’t make them in-house. In many cases, it’s the last opportunity you may have to connect with your customers. Hook them with your cuisine... finish with an incredible dessert. Don’t let them leave with a bad taste in their mouths! Our current wholesale pricing, for the 10 slice cheesecake, is $2.35 and $2.45 per slice, and there is precedent in San Diego for selling our cheesecakes at $8 and $9 per slice. So, for any menu, we have a complimentary flavor... or 2, and there is plenty of room for you to set the margins you need. Your customers will order it... it’s a proven money maker... and it’s easy!

The bottom line here, though, is that our cheesecakes are just plain good! High quality, unique, dense, and incredibly smooth. I’ve embedded some documents in the body of this email, including some company information, product availability & pricing, and profit analysis. You can also save to your computer for future reference. Please take a look, and call me with any questions or concerns. I would be happy to bring you some free samples, and supply you and your patrons with a fantastic, local product.

Thank you for your time,

Matthew
